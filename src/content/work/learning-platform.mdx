## Project Overview

EduTech Academy's learning platform needed a refresh to improve student engagement and course completion rates. The platform served both K-12 and adult learners with various courses.

## Problem

- Low course completion rate (35%)
- Students losing track of progress
- Difficult navigation between courses
- Lack of motivation and engagement

## Research

Conducted surveys with 500 students and interviews with 20 educators to understand the learning experience.

## Solution

Implemented gamification elements, progress tracking, and personalized learning paths to increase engagement and completion rates.

## Key Features

- Achievement system with badges
- Progress visualization
- Personalized course recommendations
- Interactive learning modules
- Peer discussion forums

## Results

- **60%** course completion rate (up from 35%)
- **80%** increase in daily active users
- **4.5** star average course rating
- **45%** increase in social interactions

## Takeaways

Gamification and social features significantly improve learning engagement and outcomes.
